Hard Joshi

Slide 1: Introduction

Good afternoon, everyone. My name is Hard Joshi, and I’m excited to introduce you to Altruize—a platform revolutionizing corporate giving and nonprofit fundraising. The philanthropic landscape is changing, and both charities and corporations face challenges in finding the right partners. Altruize is here to bridge that gap, making charitable giving more strategic, transparent, and impactful. Let’s dive in.

Slide 2: The Problem

The current corporate giving model is inefficient.

Charities struggle to find corporate partners that align with their mission. Companies want to contribute but lack an efficient way to identify the right charities.

Transparency and accountability remain major concerns in corporate giving. Giving has declined by nearly 10% year-over-year, making fundraising more difficult.

Competition for donations is intensifying, and fundraising costs are rising. 40% of fundraising roles remain unfilled, limiting donor outreach.

These challenges highlight the need for an innovative solution that simplifies the process for both charities and donors.

Slide 3: The Solution

Introducing Altruize – a platform that connects charities with potential donors, much like a social media network but designed for fundraising.

Powered by AI, Altruize intelligently matches companies with charities based on their missions, values, and impact goals.

This ensures that contributions are meaningful, effective, and aligned with corporate social responsibility strategies.

Slide 4: How It Works

Here’s how Altruize simplifies giving:

  • Both donors and charities create profiles outlining their missions, goals, and interests.
  • Our AI-driven matching system connects donors with charities whose values align with theirs.
  • Donors can learn more about each nonprofit, track their impact, and make direct contributions—all within the platform.
  • Altruize gamifies philanthropy, awarding points and badges to encourage engagement.

Slide 5: Benefits for Charities

For charities, Altruize offers expanded reach where AI connects nonprofits with a wider, more relevant donor base.

Improved engagement through gamification and social media integration keeps donors invested.

By reducing reliance on expensive fundraising events, we lower fundraising costs for charities.

Our analytics dashboard also helps charities optimize campaigns based on donor behaviour.

By leveraging these benefits, nonprofits can focus on what matters most—creating real impact.

Slide 6: Benefits for Companies

For corporate donors, Altruize provides AI-powered matching ensuring alignment with corporate CSR initiatives.

Companies can support causes that reflect their values and employee interests.

Enhanced engagement allows patrons to participate in fund-a-thons and corporate matching programs.

Companies can monitor their contributions and showcase social responsibility efforts effectively.

By making philanthropy more strategic and measurable, companies strengthen their brand and employee engagement.

Slide 7: Potential Drawbacks

While Altruize presents a strong value proposition, we recognize potential challenges:

  • User Adoption: Getting both charities and companies to actively use the platform can be challenging due to unfamiliarity, lack of perceived value, or resistance to change.
  • Algorithm and Accuracy Bias: The smart matching algorithm might unintentionally fail in matching certain charities or companies, leading to unfair pairings or ineffective fundraising efforts. Bias in data inputs can also skew results.
  • Data Security: Handling sensitive financial and personal data poses risks of breaches, unauthorized access, or misuse, which could damage trust in the platform.

Slide 8: Solutions to These Challenges

To address these challenges, we have clear strategies in place:

  • User Adoption:
    • Focused outreach to both charities and corporations to drive adoption.
    • Interactive tutorials, webinars, and step-by-step guides to help users navigate the platform.
    • A user-friendly interface that minimizes friction and encourages intuitive usage.
  • Algorithm Accuracy and Bias:
    • Regularly audit the algorithm’s outputs and gather user feedback to improve accuracy.
    • Train the algorithm on diverse datasets to minimize bias.
    • Allow users to adjust preferences manually for more control over match results.
  • Data Security:
    • Clear privacy settings for user data management.
    • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection laws.
    • Strong encryption protocols for data storage and transmission.

Slide 9: Conclusion

Altruize is more than just a fundraising platform—it’s a movement towards smarter, more effective philanthropy.

By seamlessly connecting charities with corporate donors, we make giving more meaningful, transparent, and impactful.

We invite you to join us in revolutionizing the way companies and nonprofits collaborate for good. Let’s build a future where giving is easier, smarter, and more engaging.

Thank you! I’d love to answer any questions.
