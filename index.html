About Altruize

Founded by industry pioneers Hard Joshi, Jayrup Nakawala, Fatema Doctor, Yogi Patel, and Shyam Jagani, Altruize is a groundbreaking platform that connects corporations with the charities that share their values. Our goal is to simplify the process of corporate giving while ensuring every donation makes a real difference.

The Altruize Solution

At its core, Altruize is a platform designed like a social network for fundraising. Leveraging advanced AI, we intelligently match companies with charities based on missions, values, and impact goals. This innovative approach ensures that each contribution is not only meaningful but also perfectly aligned with corporate social responsibility strategies.

How It Works

  • Both donors and charities create detailed profiles outlining their missions, goals, and interests.
  • Our AI-driven system connects companies with nonprofits that share common values.
  • Users can track their impact, learn about causes, and make direct contributions via our integrated platform.
  • Gamification elements such as points and badges encourage continuous engagement.

Benefits

For Charities

Altruize offers nonprofits expanded reach through AI-powered matching, enhanced donor engagement via gamification, and lower fundraising costs by reducing dependency on expensive events. Additionally, our analytics dashboard helps optimize campaigns based on donor behavior.

For Companies

Corporate donors benefit from our precision matching system that aligns contributions with their CSR initiatives. Companies can support causes that resonate with their values, engage employees through innovative programs, and showcase their social responsibility efforts to enhance brand reputation.

Addressing Challenges

While our solution is innovative, we recognize the challenges that come with change:

  • User Adoption: We are committed to engaging both charities and companies through targeted outreach, interactive tutorials, and comprehensive support.
  • Algorithm Accuracy & Bias: Our system undergoes regular audits and improvements, with diverse training data and manual controls to ensure fair and effective matching.
  • Data Security: We adhere to industry standards like GDPR and CCPA, using strong encryption and robust privacy settings to protect sensitive information.
