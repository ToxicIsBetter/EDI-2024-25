About Us

Altruize is a next-generation platform created by industry pioneers dedicated to transforming the landscape of corporate giving. We simplify the connection between businesses and nonprofits, ensuring that every donation resonates with purpose and transparency.

Our Solution

Designed like a dynamic social network for philanthropy, Altruize harnesses the power of AI to match companies with charities that share their core values. Our smart platform ensures every partnership is a win-win—driving social impact while strengthening corporate brand and engagement.

How It Works

  • Create profiles highlighting missions and goals.
  • Our AI seamlessly connects businesses with compatible nonprofits.
  • Track your impact in real time.
  • Enjoy gamified engagement with rewards and badges.

Benefits

For Charities

Boost your reach with AI-powered donor matching, engage supporters with interactive campaigns, and reduce traditional fundraising expenses. Our dashboard provides actionable insights to amplify your impact.

For Companies

Enhance your CSR initiatives by partnering with charities that align with your values. Enjoy streamlined impact tracking, employee engagement programs, and the opportunity to elevate your brand with meaningful social contributions.

Challenges & Solutions

We understand that change brings challenges. Here’s how we address them:

  • User Adoption: Through tailored outreach, immersive tutorials, and dedicated support, we ensure a smooth onboarding for both charities and corporations.
  • Algorithm Accuracy & Bias: Our AI undergoes continuous audits and refinement—leveraging diverse datasets and allowing manual adjustments for fairness.
  • Data Security: We commit to the highest standards of data protection, adhering to GDPR and CCPA, while employing robust encryption and privacy controls.
